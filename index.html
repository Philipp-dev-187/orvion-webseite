Built to replace the small tools you open every day.

Orvion is designed for people who want fewer menu bar apps, fewer background processes and one polished place for everyday Mac controls. It combines practical desktop utilities in a clean Apple-like interface without sending your private data to a server.

Everything important. Nothing bloated.

Each feature is focused on daily Mac usage: faster control, better overview and less switching between separate apps.

⌁

System usage at a glance

Monitor CPU, memory, network speed and other useful system stats directly from a clean menu bar interface. Built for quick checks without opening Activity Monitor.

▣

Window management

Move, snap and resize windows faster with keyboard shortcuts and predefined layouts. Orvion helps keep your workspace organized without dragging every window manually.

⌘

Keyboard remapping

Create practical remaps and shortcuts for your workflow. Replace awkward key combinations, trigger actions faster and make your keyboard behave the way you actually work.

◖

Notch controls

Use the MacBook notch as a useful interaction area instead of wasted space. Orvion can surface controls, status information and quick actions in a subtle, native-feeling way.

♪

Floating music player

Control your current media from a compact floating player. Keep playback, track information and quick actions visible without switching apps.

☰

Clipboard history

Recover recently copied text and snippets from a local clipboard history. Useful for writing, coding and everyday copy-paste work.

●

One app instead of many

Orvion replaces multiple single-purpose utilities with one consistent experience. Fewer icons, fewer settings panels and fewer background tools fighting for attention.

Private by default.

Orvion is planned as a local-first utility. The app does not need an account, cloud sync or a backend to provide its core features.
