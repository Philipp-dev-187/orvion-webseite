FAQ

What people usually want to know first.

Is Orvion one app or a bundle of separate tools?

It is one macOS app designed as a unified desktop companion. The goal is to replace several lightweight utilities with a more coherent experience.

Do I have to enable every feature?

No. The product is positioned as modular. You can use the notch overlay, window tools, media controls, or system stats independently.

Why does the app ask for macOS permissions?

Some features only work with macOS-level access, such as Accessibility for window actions or Camera for the mirror preview. Each request maps to a specific capability.

Does Orvion send my desktop data to the cloud?

The current marketing and legal structure assumes a local-first product with no account required for core functionality. Final legal copy should be updated to match the shipped build exactly.
